Handyman Description

Indeed.com

Handyman is a maintenance professional who performs routine maintenance on a client’s home or an office building. Clients will often call them to report a maintenance request and the Handyman will quickly arrive on-site to assess the situation and repair the issue. Some people in this role will complete quick or easy tasks, like simple repairs, managing several maintenance reports at once and cleaning facilities. They’ll often analyze a company’s appliances and equipment to detect any problems and will report these issues if they aren’t functioning properly. Handymen may also be in charge of checking different community areas to see if there are any breakdowns or mechanical failures that they can repair.
Indeed.com

JobHero

A Handyman is someone who performs a wide variety of repair and maintenance tasks. These can include things like minor electrical and plumbing fixes, painting, small remodels and construction projects, appliance repairs, and yard maintenance. A Handyman may work for a company as part of a facilities maintenance team or be self-employed, often working on a freelance basis for homeowners. According to the Bureau of Labor Statistics, demand for General Maintenance and Repair Workers, which includes Handymen, is expected to increase 8 percent through 2026.

Wikipedia

A handyman, also known as a fixer, handyperson or handyworker, is a person skilled at a wide range of repairs, typically around the home. These tasks include trade skills, repair work, maintenance work, are both interior and exterior, and are sometimes described as "side work", "odd jobs" or "fix-up tasks". Specifically, these jobs could be light plumbing jobs such as fixing a leaky toilet or light electric jobs such as changing a light fixture or bulb.
